Scheduling

Always work off the most recent information.

Our web based systems mean that, unlike any other solution on that market, all members of all collaborating teams are always working off the most recent information. Any updates are instantly reflected through all collaborating users, ensuring that everyone is always working with the most recent data.

Viewing Tasks: Gantt Charts

You choose how you want to view and manage your tasks. You can view tasks by date, by person or on a more conventional Gantt Chart. We give you options to ensure you get the job done quickly.

Finding Tasks: Integrated Searching

With our system, there are no delays. Use our quick search functions to find your tasks and associated dependencies in seconds.

Critical Path Management

MetiNet is always on the look out for changes in your project. We routinely monitor your project to detech changes in the Critical Path, and we'll notify you if any important changes occur. Rest assured, you can be safe in the knowledge that we have a handle on your Critical Path.

Budget Control  (Coming Soon...)

MetiNet takes information from all over your project in real time to ensure you know where you are with your budget.

By tracking your financial performance through our collaborative platform, you are removing the reliance on excel spreadsheets and duplicated informtion.

Our automisation of your budget allows you analyse your financial performance over the whole duration of the project, in sections or by individual tasks.

RFIs

MetiNet's unique tracking and monitoring systems ensure all RFIs within a project's lifecycle are responded to on time. Flagging systems tell the right people how long they have left to reply to an RFI, with clear audit trails showing when responses occured and what changed.

If responses are not received on time, MetiNet's logging system lead a clear path to show whose fault this was.

Clear Audit Trails

MetiNet's unique logging systems clearly show what changes occured during a project's lifecycle.

We give you clear descriptions of what was changed, when and by whom.

Tired of lengthly legal disputes? Want to see what you could have done better for next time? Let our project roadmaps show what caused you project to succeed or fail.

Clear and Concise Overviews

Analytics provided by our unique tracking and monitoring systems give you quick and fast insights into what is happening in real time with your project. MetiNet helps you understand better what your data is telling you about your project.

Project Overview

Our platform gives a well rounded project overview using graphs and tables to show you quickly where your project is up to, and what might be going wrong.

Don't waste time sifting through endless paperwork and reports. Let us do the hard work and provide you with concise visuals, enabling every collaborating party to act more quickly.

Personal Overview

Want to know quickly what is at the top of your priority list? We've got that covered.

We give you breakdowns, in order of priority, about what needs to be done with tasks associated with each collaborating user. We do all the hard work of pulling together all the information about your tasks and their dependencies, allowing you to focus on what is important.

You can even use our morning breakdown emails to help plan your day from the outset>
